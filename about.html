The IND SERVICES Story

IND SERVICES ki shuruaat 2021 mein ek chhote se sapne aur ek bade drishtikon (vision) ke sath hui thi. Founder Indresh Dangi ne dekha ki bade shehron mein toh technology tezi se badh rahi hai, lekin humare gaon aur chhote kasbon tak ye suvidhayein aaj bhi theek se nahi pahunch pa rahi hain.


"Humara sabse bada lakshya aur sankalp hai 'Gaon-gaon tak technology pahunchana'. Chahe ek kisaan ko weather ki jankari chahiye, ya kisi chhote dukandar ko apni online dukaan banani ho—hum har kadam par sath hain."

Humara mool vichar Atmanirbhar Bharat ko sach karna hai. Pichle kuch saalon mein humne kai local businesses, kisaanon aur startups ko modern digital takat pradan ki hai.

Indresh Dangi

Founder & CEO

"Technology is the bridge between a developing nation and a developed superpower. We are here to build that bridge."
